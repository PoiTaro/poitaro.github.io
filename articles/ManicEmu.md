## How to Use Manic Emu & Play Your Games

Now that you have the app, you're probably wondering where the games are. Because all App Store apps run in a "sandbox" (for your security), you can't just browse your phone. You need to import your game files using the **Files** app.

Don't worry, it's easy.

### Step 1: Get Your Legal Game Files onto Your iPhone

First, you need to get your legally dumped game files (which are probably in `.3ds` or `.cia` format) onto your iPhone.

You can do this in several ways:
* Use AirDrop from your Mac.
* Save them from an email to yourself.
* Use a cloud service like iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or Dropbox.

I find that using **iCloud Drive** is the easiest. Just save your game files to your iCloud Drive folder on your computer, and they'll be accessible in the Files app on your iPhone.

### Step 2: Import Your Games into Manic Emu

1.  Open the **Files** app on your iPhone.
2.  Navigate to where you saved your 3DS game file (e.g., in your "Downloads" or "iCloud Drive" folder).
3.  Find your game file (e.g., `MyGame.3ds`).
4.  Long-press on the file, then tap **Move**.
5.  In the "Move" menu, tap on the "On My iPhone" location.
6.  You should see a folder named **Manic Emu**. Tap it.
7.  Inside the Manic Emu folder, you might see a "Games" or "ROMs" folder. If not, just moving it into the main Manic Emu folder is fine.
8.  Tap **Copy** or **Move**.

### Step 3: Launch and Play!

Now, close the Files app and open **Manic Emu**. The app should automatically scan its own folder, find the game you just moved, and it will appear in your new game library.

Just tap the game's icon, and it will launch. It's that simple!
